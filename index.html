Animal Crossing: New Horizons
Art Simulator

This tool simulates Redd visits to figure out approximately how long it takes to get all the Animal Crossing: New Horizons Art. This tool assumes no trading, that you always have cash on hand, that villagers never give you art, and that you have perfect forgery identification skills. Hit the "Run Simulation" button to see how long it takes!
